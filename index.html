Content management should be free

Jangle is designed to be free forever. Whether you're a solo developer, or making a website for a small business, you'll be able to fully manage your content.

Integrations should be easy

Jangle makes it easy to connect to any existing GitHub project. Whether you're using Astro, Next.js, Hugo, or a custom static website, you can start managing content without leaving your browser.

Your content belongs in your hands

With Jangle, your content is stored securely in your GitHub repo. Access all the benefits of controlling your data, including the ability to use Jangle alongside existing systems and workflows.
